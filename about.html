What Makes Our Coffee Special?

The Coffee are assessed based on how they are grown,processed,their fragrance, body, aroma, acidity, and balance. If you brew a cup of specialty coffee, you'll be able to taste a drink that has multiple aromas and flavors

Specially coffees usually feature single origin arabica beans. These are the quality cousin of bitter robusta. They tend to grow higher altitudes (This is where the best coffee-growing conditions are) And taste sweeter and way way way more flavourful.
